## Exit Cap Rate Spread Guidelines

| Strategy | Typical Exit Spread vs. Entry |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Core | +0 to +25 bps |
| Core-Plus | +10 to +35 bps |
| Value-Add | +25 to +75 bps (offset by NOI growth) |
| Opportunistic | +50 to +100 bps |

**Note**: Underwriting exit cap expansion (higher exit cap than entry) is standard conservative practice. Only underwrite exit cap compression with strong market justification.
